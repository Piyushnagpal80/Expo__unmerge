About us

"The human soul is hungry for beauty and when we experience the beautiful, there is a strange sense of homecoming." Chirag's inception took place more than two decades ago. We are a prosperous trading house specializing in export of furniture and handicrafts from India. With the prime mission to offer optimum quality products to our clients our range encompasses of lamps, photoframes, wooden/iron chandeliers, Tables, Trays, Cabinets and many other small items. Backed by a rich industry experience and a dedicated team of professionals we are successfully catering to various renowned markets like

  • USA
  • China
  • Europe
  • UK
  • Canada
  • Japan
  • Australia
  • Thailand
  • South Africa
  • South America

Quality Driven

We ensure that our consignments reach the customers within the stipulated time frame. Owing to our fair dealings, we have been able to set a strong foothold in this competitive market. The production is located out of Saharanpur. Wooden art ware is manufactured, processed, packed and shipped from this unit.

Resources

We maintain huge stocks of seasoned Mango, She sham, Teak, Pine and few other types of wood and use base material as per customer's requirement. We have over 10,000 products in our collection. We have our own in-house seasoning plant.

Sampling

We also deal in glass items apart from wooden products, which are procured from Firozabad. In case new items need to be developed, we make samples based on customer's requirement as per the specifications/sketches within 96 hours.

Our organization is supported by advanced infrastructure that is spread over a large area. The facility is equipped with the latest machinery and technology that aids us in escalating our production speed without compromising the quality. To achieve the highest quality of the finished product, various technological progresses in the industry are regularly applied. Being a quality centric company, our prime focus is to make our products of optimum quality and in compliance with the global industrial norms. In order to manufacture the offered range, we use the finest raw material and the most advanced machinery under the supervision of experts.
